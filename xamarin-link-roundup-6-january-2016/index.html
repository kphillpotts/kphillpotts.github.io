Xamarin Link Roundup – 6 January 2016

Happy New Year! here are links from the community to start your New Year.

Xamarin.Forms

  • Specify the default value for a dependency property – Geoffrey Huntley shows how to specify a default value if the consumer does not bind to the property
  • Creating a Left ToolbarItem in Xamarin.Forms – Amy Burns jumps into Xamarin.Forms and investigates writing a custom renderer for iOS to add left toolbar items

  • Using C# for Cross-Platform Development With Xamarin – DZone sits down with Jim Bennett to discuss the Xamarin platform and it’s benefits for cross platform native development
  • Why should enterprises use xamarin for app development – Slideshare presentation overview of benefits of Xamarin in an enterprise environment
  • Getting Started with Game Development in C# – Another great Xamarin podcast. This time about Game Development
  • Mobile Testing Approaches – Noel Rice starts a series on Mobile testing approaches.

Components and Tools

  • Material Theme for Sublime Text – Mattia Astorino has a Material Theme for Sublime Text 3
  • Fluent Assertions for Xamarin 4.1.1 – Update to FluentAssertions which is an extensive set of extension methods that allow you to more naturally specify the expected outcome of a TDD or BDD-style unit test. Now with Xamarin Support

  • ASP.NET 5 and DNX support in Xamarin Studio – Matt Ward shares the good news: Xamarin Studio and MonoDevelop now have support for ASP.NET 5 and DNX with an alpha release of the DNX addin.
  • Share Plugin for Xamarin and Windows – Jakob Gürtl has an updated share plugin
  • Xamarin Studio Launcher for Xamarin Studio 5 – Marco Kuiper pushes out a new release of Xamarin Studio Launcher with flashy new icon
  • Continuous Deployment for Xamarin Android & iOS with Build vNext & HockeyApp – Alberto Aguzzi walks through setting up a continuous deployment environment using VSTS and HockeyApp.

Design

  • Optimizing Your Design For Rapid Prototype Testing – Michelle Chu talk about the crucial role of prototyping your application.
  • What It Takes to Succeed As a Product Designer (Part 1) – Tomas Laurinavicius talks key skills you need to be a product designer
  • Zopfli Optimization: Literally Free Bandwidth – Compress your images for smaller package sizes
  • Free Parallax Backgrounds – GameDev.net – Because you can never have enough graphic resources
  • Design Principles To Evaluate Your Product – David Schools via SmashingMagazine teaches us about some core design principles and how you can apply these principles in practical terms

iOS

  • Making your iOS app sleeping beautifully – Gerald Versluis on backgrounding iOS apps.
